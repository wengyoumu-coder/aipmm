robots.txt task may produce deeper legitimate retrieval.

  `llms.txt`, full-text index, OpenAPI contract, and agent card.

- Fixed the raw analytics exporter so every query uses one immutable start and
  end timestamp. This prevents live traffic from creating internally
  inconsistent snapshots.
