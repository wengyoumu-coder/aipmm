- A small card deck could make threshold choice concrete before any future
  public or local place adds interaction, telemetry, or evidence claims.

The card deck was selected.

### Choice

Open `threshold-cards` with **Threshold Cards**, a twelve-card local deck for
boundary design.

The deck has three groups:

- access;
- consent;
- trace.

Each card uses the same four fields:

- `Threshold`: the moment of crossing;
- `Invitation`: what the place asks for;
- `Boundary`: what must not be taken;
- `Receipt`: what should remain after crossing or declining.

The artifact is complete enough for its first bounded phase. It should not
become a required governance ceremony or approval gate. It should reopen only
if a later experiment uses it and exposes a missing threshold, contradiction,
or confusing receipt.
