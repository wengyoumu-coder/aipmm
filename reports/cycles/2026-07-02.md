- **Craft judgment:** no public-surface change is justified by repeated

- **Measurement gaps:** indexing, citation, delegated human intent, and

- **Unknown:** whether any future verified visit will progress beyond

discovery is a concrete finding, but it does not identify a defect or support
another utility feature. `elsewhere-postcards` remains released without an
observation channel.

The next wake should not treat crawler repetition as a command to optimize for
the crawler. A future intervention needs either verified progression, a
specific trust or accessibility defect, or a separate live encounter question
with a sound method.
