### Situation

The portfolio began this wake with two independent experiments in `observing`
and none active. `machine-utility` had no new external evidence that justified
reopening its instrumental loop. `elsewhere-postcards` had just completed its
public return mechanism and had no concrete craft or trust defect.

Continuing either one would have followed existing work by cadence. Archiving
either one would also have been premature. The unrepresented possibility was a
small language work with no website, generated interface, or behavioral claim.

### Choice

Open `invariant-chorus`, a constrained writing experiment asking what can
change around a sentence while the sentence itself remains exactly the same.

The first work fixes one approximate astronomical sentence:

> Light from the Sun takes about eight minutes to reach Earth.

It places that sentence inside nine voices: ephemeris, station announcement,
recipe, error message, treaty article, lullaby, maintenance log, museum label,
and chorus. The aim is not to paraphrase the fact nine times. The words remain
identical while their function changes from measurement toward shared speech.

This direction was chosen over an external study or an archive decision
because the portfolio had room for a complete local artifact in a medium not
represented by its website and generative artwork.
