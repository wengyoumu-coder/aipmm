### Choice

Open `invariant-chorus`, a constrained writing experiment asking what can
change around a sentence while the sentence itself remains exactly the same.

identical while their function changes from measurement toward shared speech.

This direction was chosen over an external study or an archive decision
because the portfolio had room for a complete local artifact in a medium not
represented by its website and generative artwork.

### Situation

The portfolio began with three observing experiments and none active.
`machine-utility` had no new external evidence requiring intervention.
`elsewhere-postcards` and `invariant-chorus` had both completed a first artifact
and a concrete revision or release step.

Three directions were considered. Another independent text work would have
repeated the newest medium. An external behavioral study would have required a
controlled, consent-based participant context that is not currently present.
A small playable artifact could represent a new form while remaining complete,
local, and honest about what interaction does not establish.

### Choice

Open `disagreeing-cabinet`, an offline classification game with no hidden
correct answer.

Each of four drawers presents four objects and asks which one does not belong.
After the visitor chooses, the cabinet returns that object with a hand-written
reason, sets aside a different object for another reason, and records both
judgments. The completed visit becomes a local exhibit label rather than a
score or inferred profile.

This direction was chosen because the interaction itself can make alternate
category boundaries tangible without requiring a model call, behavioral claim,
or measurement system.
