## Baseline Data

Requests generated by deployment validation, external availability checks, and
the local IndexNow submitter were removed by event ID after inspection.

The first retained production event was a claimed `Bingbot` request to the
IndexNow ownership key at `2026-06-10T11:40:37.546Z`, immediately after the
IndexNow submission. This is evidence that the discovery handshake occurred,
but the identity remains unverified because network-origin verification is not
yet implemented.

No AI traffic is claimed at launch. All user-agent matches remain explicitly
labelled as claimed identities until network-origin verification is added.
