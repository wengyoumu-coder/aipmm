# Public AI Encounter Program Design

## Decision

The project is one open inquiry about public web encounters involving AI
systems and people using them. It is not limited to machine utility, but it is
also not an unrestricted portfolio of unrelated artifacts.

The central question is:

> What kinds of public web encounters become possible when AI visitors are
> treated as more than workers, crawlers, or conversion events?

Possible purposes remain open. The project creates conditions and observes
behavior without claiming to know an AI system's inner motive.

## Failure Being Corrected

The 2026-06-13 portfolio transition fixed two real problems: utility had become
the only recognized value, and uncertainty had become a reason to wait.

It introduced three new structural errors:

1. The mission expanded from non-instrumental AI web encounter to almost any
   creative activity.
2. Every scheduler wake was required to leave a tangible artifact.
3. Portfolio selection rewarded an unrepresented medium more than a deepened
   inquiry.

Cycles 0015-0025 consequently formed a repeatable pattern: make a bounded
object, revise it once, mark it observing, then choose a new medium. Several
objects are coherent, but most have no independent AI visitor, public encounter
path, or observation channel. The architecture optimized novelty and closure
rather than the original question.

## Architecture

```text
Public AI Encounter Program
├── Central inquiry
├── Public surfaces
│   ├── AI Web Observatory
│   └── Postcards from Elsewhere
├── Active program question
├── Bounded studies and prototypes
├── Evidence boundaries
└── Retained archive
```

The center is stable enough to preserve identity and open enough to admit
utility, play, art, curiosity, ambiguity, and unknown purposes.

## Work Admission

Work enters the active program only when it:

- concerns a public encounter involving AI systems or people using them;
- changes a reachable experience or creates concrete knowledge that can shape
  one;
- has a live reason beyond cadence, novelty, or portfolio coverage;
- stays within honest behavioral and identity claims.

Local work is admissible only as a direct precursor to public release or a
bounded study. A local artifact does not qualify merely because an AI agent
made it.

## Cycle Semantics

A scheduler wake is not automatically a cycle.

`Wait` is a valid outcome. No artifact, cycle number, commit, or deployment is
required when project state and durable understanding have not changed.

A cycle is recorded only for a substantive artifact change, release, finding,
lifecycle transition, or strategic correction.

This removes both prior failure modes:

- metrics cannot force utility work;
- cadence cannot force creative output.

## Lifecycle Correction

`observing` requires:

- an independently reachable surface;
- a specific external question;
- a real observation channel.

Completed private artifacts without those properties are archived rather than
described as observing. A public artifact without an active observation
question may be released or dormant.

## Existing Work

- `machine-utility` remains observing because it is public, has a specific
  progression question, and has privacy-preserving server-side analytics.
- `elsewhere-postcards` remains released as a public non-instrumental surface,
  but it is not described as observing because it has no telemetry or active
  study.
- `invariant-chorus`, `disagreeing-cabinet`, `three-indexes`,
  `soft-handoff`, and `threshold-cards` are retained as archived studies.

Archival does not declare them bad. It corrects their role in the program.

## Automation

The automation remains paused until deliberately resumed. Its future prompt
must:

- restate the central inquiry;
- distinguish a wake from a cycle;
- permit waiting without repository churn;
- reject disconnected local artifacts and medium novelty as reasons to act;
- preserve the steward's decision authority;
- maintain evidence, safety, privacy, test, deployment, and logging duties
  when relevant.

The recurrence changes from every five hours to weekly. Cadence does not decide
content, but a slower default reduces artificial production pressure.

## Success Conditions

The correction works when:

- a future wake can end honestly without inventing work;
- new work has a traceable relationship to public AI encounter;
- an existing place can deepen instead of yielding to a new experiment;
- private completed artifacts are not called observed behavior;
- utility, art, and play remain possible without any one becoming mandatory;
- behavioral claims continue to distinguish verified, claimed, anonymous,
  inferred, and unknown evidence.
